Spaghetti Casserole

A Midwestern grandma specialty, with lots of cheese baked on top!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Spaghetti Noodles
  • 1 lb Ground beef
  • One can stewed tomatoes
  • One can cream of mushroom soup
  • Chopped onion
  • Chopped green pepper
  • American cheese slices
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees
  2. Brown ground beef
  3. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions
  4. Once noodles are cooked, combine remaining ingredients except cheese slices in a 9x13 baking dish
  5. Layer cheese slices on top of casserole, slightly overlapping the edges
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is bubbly and melted
